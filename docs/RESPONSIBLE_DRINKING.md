# Responsible drinking — product positioning

CheekyPint is a **social drinking diary and pub-memory app**, not a system that encourages people
to drink as much as possible. This positioning is a hard product requirement, not a marketing line.

## What we do

- Require legal-age confirmation before use (not pre-checked, recorded as `legal_age_confirmed_at`).
- Default to an 18+ experience while stating users must meet their **local** legal drinking age.
- Show a responsible-use statement during onboarding.
- Replace celebration with a **welfare notice** after clustered entries: *"Take it easy. Have some
  water and look after yourself."* (`WelfareMonitor`, tested).
- Keep a permanent **Drink responsibly** link in Settings.
- Let users hide all quantities from friends and keep a private diary.
- Use neutral language: "Friend standings", "Pints recorded", "Pub regulars".

## What we never do

No drinking challenges · no speed-drinking mechanics · no "drink more" notifications · no
dangerous streaks · no unlimited-consumption achievements · no countdowns between drinks · no
public/global rankings · no rewards for high quantities · no celebratory animations after unusually
high consumption · no intoxication diagnosis or medical claims.

## Language guardrails

Avoid, in-app and in marketing: "drink the most", "beat your mates", "become the biggest drinker",
"never stop", "another round", "ultimate drinking competition".

## Not medical advice

The app doesn't provide medical advice. The in-app responsible-drinking screen points users who
are worried about their drinking to a GP or local support service.
