## 1. Scope

This policy explains how CheekyPint (the "App") processes personal data under the EU General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Austrian law.

## 2. Data we process

- **Account:** authentication identifier and email address (including Apple private-relay email).
- **Profile:** display name; optional username, bio, avatar, and broad city/country.
- **Diary:** drinks you log (serving type, time, alcohol-free flag, optional private note),
  optional pub selections and visit history.
- **Social:** friends, blocks, and reports you create.
- **Device/location:** precise location **only** while you actively use nearby-pub search
  (When-In-Use); it is not stored or shared. We do not use background or "Always" location.
- **Usage analytics:** minimal, identifier-free product events, disabled by default.

We do **not** collect contacts, browsing history, financial or health data, or advertising IDs.

## 3. Purposes & legal bases

| Purpose | Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6) |
|---------|---------------------------|
| Provide the App (auth, diary, friends, standings) | Contract (6(1)(b)) |
| Keep the service safe (moderation, rate limits, abuse prevention) | Legitimate interests (6(1)(f)) |
| Optional analytics to improve the App | Consent (6(1)(a)), where required |
| Legal compliance (e.g. lawful requests) | Legal obligation (6(1)(c)) |

Age confirmation is required; we default to an 18+ experience and you must meet your local legal
drinking age.

## 4. Sharing

Only with **accepted friends**, and only the fields your privacy settings permit. There is no
public visibility and no global leaderboard. We do **not** sell personal data or run targeted ads.

## 5. Processors & international transfers

- **Supabase** — hosting, authentication, database, storage ([[region/DPA reference]]).
- **Apple** — Sign in with Apple.

Where data is processed outside the EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards ([[SCCs / adequacy]]).

## 6. Retention

We keep data while your account is active. On deletion we anonymise and remove data per our
[Data Retention Policy](DATA_RETENTION_POLICY.md). Backups roll off within [[retention window]].

## 7. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and withdrawal of consent.
Use in-app export and **Settings → Delete account**, or contact [[privacy@cheekypint.app]]. You may
lodge a complaint with the Austrian DPA (Datenschutzbehörde) or your local authority.

## 8. Security

See our security practices summary. No method is perfectly secure, but we apply Row Level Security,
least-privilege backend functions, encryption in transit, and Keychain storage of credentials.

## 9. Children

CheekyPint is for adults of legal drinking age. It is not directed at children.

## 10. Changes

We will post changes here and update the effective date; material changes will be notified in-app.
