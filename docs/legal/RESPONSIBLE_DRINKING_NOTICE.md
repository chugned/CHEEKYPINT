# Responsible Drinking Notice — CheekyPint

> **Template requiring review by a qualified legal professional before production launch.**
> This is not medical advice.

CheekyPint is a **social diary and pub-memory app**. It is not a drinking game and does not
encourage you to drink more.

## Please drink responsibly

- Know your limits and pace yourself.
- Alternate alcoholic drinks with water; don't drink on an empty stomach.
- **Never drink and drive**, and never let a mate drive after drinking.
- If drinking stops being fun, it's completely okay to stop.
- Look out for your friends.

## What this app does and doesn't do

- It records drinks **you choose to log**; totals are self-reported and are **not** a medical or
  legal measurement of intoxication.
- It has no challenges, streaks, "drink more" prompts, countdowns, or global rankings.
- After several drinks logged in a short time, it replaces any celebration with a gentle reminder
  to take it easy and have some water.
