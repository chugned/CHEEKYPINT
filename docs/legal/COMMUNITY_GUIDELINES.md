# Community Guidelines — CheekyPint

> **Template requiring review by a qualified legal professional before production launch.**

CheekyPint is a friendly, grown-up place to remember good nights at the pub. Keep it that way.

## Do

- Be respectful and kind to your mates.
- Keep your profile photo and text appropriate and truthful.
- Drink responsibly and look after each other.

## Don't

- Harass, bully, threaten, or hate on anyone.
- Impersonate other people or brands.
- Post inappropriate, explicit, unlawful, or misleading profile content.
- Encourage dangerous drinking, drink-driving, or drinking games.
- Try to inflate totals, abuse friend codes, or misuse the service.

## Safety

CheekyPint is a diary, not a challenge. We never reward or celebrate heavy or rapid drinking. If
you're worried about your own or a friend's drinking, please reach out to a GP or a local support
service. See the [Responsible Drinking Notice](RESPONSIBLE_DRINKING_NOTICE.md).

## Reporting & enforcement

Use **Block** and **Report** on any profile. Reports go to our moderation queue. We may remove
content, disable profiles, or suspend accounts that break these guidelines — especially for
harassment, impersonation, inappropriate imagery, or underage-use concerns.

**Contact:** [[support@cheekypint.app]].
